Proven Successes

Automating Executive Financial Reports

Turn complex KPI data into instant, comprehensive reports.

Client: SBB

Challenge

Every department required manual analysis of hundreds of financial KPIs to write a monthly report.

Solution

We implemented an LLM-based report generation system, powered by a data pipeline and feature engineering, to automatically analyse financial KPIs and generate the executive report.

Impact

Time savings and the generation of additional insights. The client recognized the collaboration as a "transformative experience" in leveraging AI for data analytics.

Achieving 80% Time Savings in Accounting

Revolutionizing document processing with hybrid AI.

Client: Fiduciary

Challenge

Accountants had to manually divide large PDF scans into individual invoices, interpret positions, and often perform web research to suggest postings.

Solution

A hybrid AI System (fine-tuned LLM + vision + OCR) was developed that automatically splits documents, classifies them, extracts data into a standardized JSON format, and suggests postings.

Impact

Successful autonomous processing of over 95% of documents, resulting in time savings of 80%.

AI-Powered Candidate Search and Vetting

Accelerate recruitment by identifying the perfect fit.

Client: Amrop

Challenge

An AI system to support candidate search, efficiently identifying and evaluating candidates against a complex ideal profile.

Solution

Developed an AI system utilizing data enrichment through Webresearch AI. The system evaluates candidates based on the degree of fulfilment against an ideal profile.

Impact

A shortlist of suitable candidates is generated, complete with a rating (e.g., 76% fulfillment) and analysis of sources (e.g., LinkedIn, Company Website).

Instant Access to Complex Legal Knowledge (RAG)

Semantic search instead of keyword hunting.

Client: Tax Consultancy

Challenge

Tax legal knowledge was scattered across many sources (online libraries, internal documents), making search time-consuming.

Solution

Aggregation of all legal documents into a centralized vector database (RAG solution). An LLM-based chat allows users to query legal information and receive answers to complex questions.

Impact

Massive time savings, centralized and fast access to knowledge, and the ability to use semantic search rather than simple keyword search.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ensure data privacy and security?

We prioritize data security above all else. Our solutions can be deployed on-premise or in your private cloud, ensuring you retain full control over your data. We adhere to strict data protection protocols and can comply with industry-specific regulations like GDPR and HIPAA.

What kind of ROI can we expect?

Return on investment varies by project but is typically measured in productivity gains, cost savings, and quality improvements. For example, automating report generation can save hundreds of hours annually, while AI-powered customer support can reduce overhead and improve customer satisfaction. We work with you to define and track key metrics from the start.

How long does a typical project take?

Our "Proof of Value" phase is designed to be quick, typically taking 2-4 weeks. This allows us to demonstrate tangible results with a functional prototype. A full-scale integration and deployment can range from 2 to 6 months, depending on the complexity and your existing infrastructure.
